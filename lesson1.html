Lesson 1: Introduction to the Guitar

Welcome to Lesson 1! In this lesson, we'll cover the fundamental aspects of the guitar to get you started on your musical journey.

Parts of the Guitar

The guitar has various parts, each contributing to its sound and playability. Familiarize yourself with the main components:

  • Body
  • Neck
  • Fretboard
  • Headstock
  • Strings

    • Guitar parts Diagram

How to Hold the Guitar

Proper posture and holding the guitar are crucial for comfortable playing. Follow these guidelines:

  • Sit up straight with the guitar on your right leg (for right-handed players).
  • Rest the body of the guitar on your leg.
  • Hold the neck of the guitar with your non-dominant hand.
  • Use a guitar strap for standing play.

Introduction to Tuning

Before you start playing, it's essential to have your guitar properly tuned. Use an electronic tuner or reference pitch to tune each string to the correct pitch.
