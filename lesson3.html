Lesson 3: Play a Simple Song

Congratulations on reaching Lesson 3! Now, let's put those basic chords into practice and play a simple song. We'll use the chords you learned in Lesson 2 to play "Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star."

Chords Used in the Song

We'll use the following chords:

  • C Major (C)
  • G Major (G)
  • A Minor (Am)
  • F Major (F)
