Lesson 4: Essential Guitar Techniques

Welcome to Lesson 4! In this lesson, we'll explore some essential guitar techniques that will enhance your playing skills. These techniques include strumming, fingerpicking, and understanding rhythm.

1. Strumming

Strumming is a fundamental technique where you use a pick or your fingers to brush across the strings. Practice strumming patterns with different rhythms to add dynamics to your playing.

2. Fingerpicking

Fingerpicking involves plucking the strings with your fingers instead of using a pick. Learn basic fingerpicking patterns and exercises to improve coordination and finger strength.

3. Understanding Rhythm

Rhythm is a crucial aspect of playing the guitar. Learn how to read rhythm notation, count beats, and understand time signatures. This knowledge will help you play with precision and in various musical styles.
