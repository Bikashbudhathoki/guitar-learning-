Lesson 2: Basic Guitar Chords

Welcome to Lesson 2! In this lesson, we'll dive into some basic guitar chords. Chords are the building blocks of many songs, and they are essential for every guitarist to learn.

Understanding Guitar Chords

A chord is a group of notes played together. Guitar chords are formed by pressing down on the strings at specific frets. We'll start with some common open chords.

Common Open Chords

Here are a few basic open chords to get you started:

  • C Major (C)
  • G Major (G)
  • D Major (D)
  • A Major (A)
  • E Major (E)
